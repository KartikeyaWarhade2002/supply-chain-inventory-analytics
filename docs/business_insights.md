# Business Insights

## Insight 1 — Overall Supply Chain Performance

**Business Question:** What is the overall performance of the supply chain?

**Finding:** The business processed **65,752 orders** from **20,652 customers**, generating **$33.05M** in revenue and **$3.97M** in profit, resulting in an overall **profit margin of 12.00%**.

**Recommendation:** Continue monitoring profitability while optimizing operational costs to improve overall profit margins.

---

## Insight 2 — Product Category Performance

**Business Question:** Which product categories generate the highest revenue?

**Finding:** **Fishing** is the highest revenue-generating category with **$6.23M** in revenue, followed by **Cleats ($3.98M)** and **Camping & Hiking ($3.70M)**.

**Recommendation:** Prioritize inventory planning and promotional campaigns for top-performing categories while evaluating growth opportunities for lower-performing categories.

---

## Insight 3 — Market Performance

**Business Question:** Which markets contribute the most revenue?

**Finding:** **Europe** generated the highest revenue (**$9.77M**), closely followed by **LATAM ($9.24M)**. The **USCA** market achieved the highest profit margin (**12.39%**).

**Recommendation:** Continue investing in high-performing markets while identifying strategies to increase market share in regions with lower revenue.

---

## Insight 4 — Shipping Mode Analysis

**Business Question:** How do different shipping modes affect business performance?

**Finding:** **Standard Class** accounts for the highest number of orders (**39,324**) and generates **$19.79M** in revenue. However, **First Class** records a **100% late delivery rate**, indicating potential operational issues.

**Recommendation:** Review logistics processes for premium shipping services and investigate the causes of delayed First Class deliveries.

---

## Insight 5 — Monthly Revenue Trend

**Business Question:** How does revenue change throughout the year?

**Finding:** Revenue remains relatively stable throughout the year, peaking in **January ($3.11M)** before gradually declining toward **November** and **December**.

**Recommendation:** Analyze seasonal demand patterns and introduce targeted promotions during lower-performing months to maintain consistent revenue.

---

## Insight 6 — Shipping Performance

**Business Question:** How efficiently are customer orders delivered?

**Finding:** **Late deliveries** account for **37,698 orders**, representing the largest delivery group, while **Early deliveries** generate nearly **$7.98M** in revenue.

**Recommendation:** Improve logistics planning and warehouse operations to reduce late deliveries and enhance customer satisfaction.

---

## Insight 7 — Customer Segment Performance

**Business Question:** Which customer segments generate the highest business value?

**Finding:** The **Consumer** segment contributes the highest revenue (**$17.16M**) and profit (**$2.07M**), while maintaining an average order value of **$183.57**.

**Recommendation:** Focus marketing campaigns on the Consumer segment while developing strategies to increase revenue from Corporate and Home Office customers.

---

## Insight 8 — Top Product Performance

**Business Question:** Which products generate the highest revenue?

**Finding:** **Field & Stream Sportsman 16 Gun Fire Safe** is the highest revenue-generating product (**$6.23M**), followed by **Perfect Fitness Perfect Rip Deck** (**$3.97M**).

**Recommendation:** Maintain inventory availability for high-performing products and use their sales patterns to guide product assortment and demand forecasting.
